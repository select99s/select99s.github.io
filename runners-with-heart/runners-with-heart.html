About Our Club

Runners With Heart is a welcoming running community for all experience levels. Whether you are training for your first 5K or your next marathon, we offer friendly support, structured training, and a mission centered on lifelong cardiovascular wellness.

Training Programs

Beginner 5K

8-week plan focused on confidence, consistency, and injury prevention.

Half Marathon Prep

12-week progression with pace sessions, long runs, and recovery support.

Heart-Healthy Walk/Run

Low-impact group sessions designed for active recovery and wellness goals.
